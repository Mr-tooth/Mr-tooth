# Junhang Lai

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology, working on motion planning and control for humanoid and legged robots. I am currently a research intern at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou).

My research covers humanoid locomotion, whole-body control, reinforcement learning, and sim-to-real deployment. I am especially interested in combining model-based optimization with learning-based control for reliable robot motion in real environments.

## Research and Engineering

- Humanoid and wheel-biped locomotion planning and control
- Reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and motion retargeting
- Whole-body control, MPC, NMPC, and trajectory optimization
- Robot dynamics simulation, system integration, and real-robot deployment
- Embodied intelligence and cerebrum-cerebellum coordinated control

## Open Source

I maintain robotics software and reusable engineering tools through [BitRoboticsLab](https://github.com/bitroboticslab). My public projects focus on robot motion, simulation, control, and deployment workflows.
